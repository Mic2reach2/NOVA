Comprehensive Eye Examination & Refractive Error Correction

Our comprehensive eye examinations assess both your vision and overall eye health using modern diagnostic equipment. We accurately identify refractive errors and underlying eye conditions.

We diagnose and correct:

  • Short-sightedness (Myopia)
  • Long-sightedness (Hyperopia)
  • Astigmatism
  • Presbyopia (age-related near vision difficulty)

Correction options include:

  • Prescription reading and bifocal lenses
  • Progressive lenses for seamless near-to-distance vision
  • Visual performance optimization

Clear vision begins with accurate diagnosis.

Occupational Eye Care & Workplace Visual Screening

We specialize in occupational eye care services for individuals, companies, and organizations where vision plays a critical role in safety, productivity, and performance.

Our occupational services include:

  • Pre-employment eye screening
  • Periodic workplace vision assessments
  • Visual fitness evaluations for safety-critical roles
  • Digital eye strain assessments
  • Industrial and corporate eye health programs

Ideal for:

  • Office workers
  • Industrial staff
  • Drivers and machine operators
  • Oil & gas workers
  • Security personnel

Healthy vision improves productivity, safety, and efficiency.

Anti-Glare & Computer Vision Glasses

Prolonged exposure to digital screens can lead to eye strain, headaches, blurred vision, dry eyes, and reduced productivity. We provide customized computer vision solutions to protect your eyes during extended screen use.

Benefits include:

  • Reduced digital eye strain
  • Improved visual comfort and clarity
  • Reduced headaches and eye fatigue
  • Enhanced focus during screen use

Recommended for:

  • Office workers
  • Students
  • Programmers
  • Content creators
  • Remote workers

Prescription Safety Glasses for High-Risk Jobs

We dispense certified prescription safety glasses designed to protect your eyes in hazardous environments without compromising visual clarity.

Suitable for:

  • Construction sites
  • Factories and workshops
  • Oil & gas industry
  • Laboratories
  • Welding and engineering environments

Features include:

  • Impact-resistant lenses
  • Protective, durable frames
  • Prescription customization
  • Compliance with safety standards

See clearly. Work safely.

Diagnosis & Treatment of Eye Diseases

Early detection is key to preventing vision loss. We diagnose and manage a wide range of eye diseases and conditions using evidence-based care.

  • Eye infections
  • Dry eye disease
  • Allergic eye conditions
  • Glaucoma screening and monitoring
  • Cataract assessment and referral
  • Red and painful eye conditions
  • Uveitis and inflammatory eye conditions

Where advanced care or surgery is required, we provide prompt referrals to qualified ophthalmologists.
